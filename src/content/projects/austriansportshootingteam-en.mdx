Anyone seeking information had to actively visit the website. The desire for an Instagram or Facebook presence had existed for years - but repeatedly failed due to budget limitations and the challenge of representing all disciplines equally.

## Implementation

I took over the existing Facebook page, set up a new Instagram and Meta Business account, and created a custom Canva template along with a cloud-based image workflow. In coordination with the athletes, we decided which competition results would be shared.

As a launch campaign, we organized a giveaway in cooperation with **Trainingsbuddy**: the prize was a training session with **Martin Strempfl** and **Bernhard Pickl** - a format that generated high reach and engagement.

In preparation for the 2024 Olympic Games, I updated the template and collaborated with **Team Austria** (Olympic team). Starting with the 2024/25 season, I also began designing schedules, final announcements, and a team logo.
