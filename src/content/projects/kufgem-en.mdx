**Period:**  
- **05–07/2020:** Mandatory internship (FH Kufstein)  
- **08/2020–06/2021:** Employed as a web developer at **Kufgem GmbH** (left after being accepted into the Austrian Armed Forces elite sports programme)

**Tech/Tools:** GEM2GO (RIS GmbH), SCSS, TypeScript/jQuery

## In a nutshell (as part of the Kufgem team)  
I designed mockups for municipalities, implemented them in the **GEM2GO** CMS and supported the roll-out. This included configuration (incl. the app), **training** for editors, **sub-sites** for municipal facilities, **accessibility checks & training**, and ongoing **support/helpdesk**.

## Responsibilities (selection)  
- **Mockups/redesigns** for new and existing clients; close collaboration through to sign-off  
- **Implementation** of designs in **GEM2GO** and roll-out to the new site  
- **Configuration**, including the **GEM2GO app**  
- **Training** for website editors (main sites and sub-areas)  
- **Sub-areas/sub-sites** for facilities (e.g. works yard, nursery): dedicated navigation/colour scheme/logo, roles & permissions in the CMS; incl. staff training  
- **Helpdesk/support:** handling client tickets and questions  
- **Accessibility checks (WCAG)** incl. action plans, implementation & client reviews; delivered an **accessibility training**  
- Additionally: **Lost & found / “NovaFind” (Rubicon)** — setup and support
